About

Cam Eickmeyer is a playwright and novelist whose work explores relationships, family, identity, and the unexpected moments that shape people's lives.

His plays have been produced by theatre companies and festivals, including Arts Uprise Festival in Colorado Springs. His work has been recognized for its focus on character-driven storytelling, emotional honesty, and the complexities of human connection.

Cam is a member of the Dramatists Guild and writes both stage plays and fiction. His projects include short plays, novels, and children's literature.

He lives in Colorado with his family and continues to write stories for the stage and the page.
