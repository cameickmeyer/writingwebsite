Stories for the Stage, Screen, and Page

Whether writing a ten-minute play, feature screenplay, novel, or poem, I'm drawn to stories that explore relationships, identity, impossible choices, and the moments that reshape ordinary lives. My work ranges from dark comedy and thrillers to speculative fiction, heartfelt drama, satire, and children's literature.

Career Highlights

  • Produced by theatres and festivals across the United States and internationally.
  • Published by Mushroom Cloud Press, Falling Star Magazine, Three Line Poetry and CommuterLit.
  • Feature screenplays optioned and recognized by Coverfly and Barnstorm Media competitions.
  • Member of the Dramatists Guild.
  • Licensing available for educational, community, and professional productions.
