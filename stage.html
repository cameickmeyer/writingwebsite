Exit Poll

Three reporters desperate for an election day scoop, post up outside a voting location willing to do anything, yes, absolutely anything, to predict the outcome of the votes.

What They're Saying: Tom Moran says " A fast-paced, chaotic, very funny look at a modern election day. Does a great job of skewering pretty much everyone without avoiding some of the land mines of writing about politics."

Stay Calm and Panic

Two friends try to navigate the Covid-19 pandemic, but a pesky CDC Agent keeps getting in the way.

What They're Saying: Kally Anderson says "It so perfectly captures the whiplash, the misinformation, and the ridiculously impotent way we got by during quarantine. It builds so many delightful beats of both physical and verbal comedy, and it calls for a chaotic transformation of the stage. The audience was eating it up because it really just was the perfect lampoon of that time."
