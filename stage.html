Heaven or Hell

5 minute or less monologue: A dad escapes to the bathroom to hide from his son's question: Who goes to heaven or hell in Gaza right now?

What They're Saying: Paul Smith says "This extremely pertinent piece may refer to the conflict in Gaza in 2023, but it could refer to any conflict. Explaining the highly complex in simpler terms to a child is almost impossible - war is never straightforward, nor is apportioning blame. What Cam Eickmeyer does here is vocalise the difficulties involved in this stark, but utterly truthful monologue, which, in its simplicity, gives it great power and dynamism. Incredibly effective writing. "

2024 Original Monologue Contest - Talking Horse Productions
