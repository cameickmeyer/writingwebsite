Welcome

Thank you for visiting my website.

I'm a playwright and author based in Colorado. I write stories that explore the choices people make, the relationships that define us, and the moments that challenge what we believe about ourselves and each other. My work ranges from heartfelt dramas and thoughtful comedies to children's literature and fiction.

Whether you're a theatre director looking for your next production, an actor searching for compelling material, or a reader interested in my latest projects, I hope you'll find something here that resonates with you.
