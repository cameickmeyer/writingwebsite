Cam Eickmeyer

Cam Eickmeyer writes plays that explore technology, social justice, and ethical tension, often through dark humor. His work has been produced on stage and selected for festivals across the United States, and has also been produced as audio drama, including internationally in the UK. His fiction and poetry have appeared in various outlets, and his screenplays have been optioned. A former journalist, he lives in Colorado and is a member of the Dramatists Guild of America since 2023.
