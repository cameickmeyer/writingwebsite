Performance Licensing

Thank you for your interest in producing one of my plays. I am always excited to work with theatres, schools, community organizations, and other groups looking to bring new work to the stage.

Licensing Fees

Performance licenses begin at $50. The final fee depends on factors such as the type of organization, number of performances, and venue size.

I proudly offer discounted licensing rates for school theatre programs, colleges and universities, and other educational organizations.

How to Request a License

To request performance rights, please use the contact form and include:

  • The title of the play
  • Name of your theatre or organization
  • Performance dates
  • Performance location
  • Number of planned performances
  • Any additional information you'd like to share

Questions?

If you're unsure which play is right for your organization or have questions about licensing, I'd be happy to help. Please visit the Contact page to get in touch.
